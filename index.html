Dr Sunil Kumar Sharma
Member of Federal Parliament of Nepal

Born on 2034 ,Poush 22 (January 6, 1978), in Jhapa,Damak Dumse Dr. Sunil Kumar Sharma is a Nepali Congress politician and a Member of Parliament representing Morang Constituency No. 3. Active in politics since 2054 BS, he has served as a Mahasamiti member of the party since its 13th General Convention.
